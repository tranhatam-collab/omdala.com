# Omdala Brand Audit

## Date: 2026-04-05

### Overview
This document provides an audit for the brand Omdala ensuring all branding aligns with the current market standards and expectations.

### Key Areas of Focus
- **Logo Usage**: Verify that the logo is used consistently across all platforms.
- **Color Scheme**: Ensure the correct hex codes for brand colors are being adhered to.
- **Typography**: Review if the chosen fonts are being used consistently.

### Recommendations
1. **Consistent Logo Application**: Ensure usage guidelines are followed to maintain brand integrity.
2. **Color Consistency**: Regular audits to check for adherence to color guidelines.
3. **Font Usage**: Maintain font consistency in all marketing materials.

### Conclusion
To maintain a strong brand presence, compliance with these standards is crucial.

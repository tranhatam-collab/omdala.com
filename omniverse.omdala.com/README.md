# AI Omniverse Workspace

AI Omniverse is the physical reality and environment-control product inside the OMDALA ecosystem.

This workspace is reserved for the Omniverse-specific stack:

- device onboarding and registry
- rooms and spaces
- scenes and automation
- gateway runtime
- physical status, alerts, and proof
- future Matter, IoT, office, and property orchestration

## Position in the ecosystem

- `OMDALA` = motherbrand and shared platform
- `Om AI` = AI human call, learning, communication, business interaction
- `AI Omniverse` = spaces, devices, scenes, gateways, physical action

## Hard rules

Do not place these Om AI features here:

- lesson systems
- teacher personas
- language-learning flows
- family/school learning dashboards
- receptionist or sales roleplay flows

Those belong to `om-ai.omdala.com/`.
